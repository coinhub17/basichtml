Kerio Valley Nation

"A Sovereign Micronation Advancing Prosperity, Heritage, and Governance"

The Kerio Valley Nation is a modern sovereign micronation situated in the heart of the Kerio Valley Highlands. Governed under the leadership of the Commander-in-Chief and overseen by the Kerio Valley Council, the Nation is committed to the principles of investment, heritage preservation, and structured governance. The Nation maintains diplomatic representation abroad, fostering international partnerships, economic growth, and cultural exchange. Citizens and investors are welcomed into a thriving national framework, where formal recognition, land ownership, and official documentation are processed with utmost diligence and ceremonial precision.
